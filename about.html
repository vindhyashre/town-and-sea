Our Story

Town & Sea is a boutique online auction and consignment marketplace specializing in unique antique pieces and pre-loved treasures.

Founded in 2020, we've cultivated a carefully curated collection of distinctive pieces that tell their own stories. Our mission is to connect discerning collectors with extraordinary finds while ensuring each piece finds its perfect home.

We believe in the beauty of preservation and the joy of discovery. Each item in our collection is thoroughly authenticated and lovingly presented, allowing its history and craftsmanship to shine through.
